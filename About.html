Our Elegant Identity

Our Story

From Humble Beginnings

Aurielle of Oré & Argenté is a sibling-owned jewellery boutique born from a story of culture, family, and timeless elegance. Founded by four sisters Ayreen, Nurahin, Izzah, and Qys. Our brand reflects both the beauty of heritage and the bond we share as family. Our name is inspired by the French words “oré” (gold) and “argenté” (silver), symbolizing the metals we specialize in gold and silver jewellery, crafted with grace and intention. The French influence is a tribute to our father, who is from France, and our Malaysian mother, whose warmth and strength inspire our style and values. Together, their story gave rise to us a blend of cultures, traditions, and love. Every piece in our collection is carefully curated or designed to speak elegance with meaning from rings and bracelets to earrings, necklaces, and watches. We aim to offer jewellery that feels personal, refined, and empowering. Welcome to our world where family, culture, and beauty come together.

Our Mission

Our mission is to celebrate individuality and heritage through exquisite gold and silver jewellery. We strive to create timeless pieces that honor tradition, empower self-expression, and become cherished symbols for every generation. Each creation is a testament to our passion for craftsmanship, elegance, and meaningful connections.

Looking Forward

As we continue to grow, our commitment remains rooted in quality, creativity, and community. Thank you for being part of our journey.
